    Discover Destinations

    Tungnath:It is the highest Shiva temple in the world, situated at an altitude of about 3,680 meters (12,073 feet) in the Garhwal Himalayas, and is part of the Panch Kedar pilgrimage circuit.

    Valley Of Flowers:The Valley of Flowers in Uttarakhand is a stunning national park renowned for its vibrant alpine meadows and diverse flora, attracting nature lovers and trekkers from around the world.
    Chatpal:Chatpal is a picturesque, off-the-beaten-path village in Jammu and Kashmir, India, known for its serene natural beauty, lush greenery, and tranquil environment, offering a perfect escape for nature enthusiasts.
    Chandrashila: a peak in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India, offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Himalayas and is a popular trekking destination often combined with a visit to the Tungnath temple.
    Mount Kilash:Mount Kailash captivates with its sublime beauty, a towering testament to nature's grandeur, adorned with pristine snow-capped peaks.
    Lonavla:Lonavla, nestled in the Sahyadri range of Maharashtra, India, is a charming hill station renowned for its lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and misty landscapes, offering a refreshing retreat for travelers seeking respite from the bustling city life.
